WaveFake Datasheet
==================
Motivation  : Facilitate research on audio deepfake detection
Composition : 17 original LJSpeech utterances (real/) +
              17 HiFi-GAN re-synthesised counterparts (fake/)
              This is a subset; full dataset: 104,885 clips.
Generator   : HiFi-GAN vocoder (ljspeech_hifiGAN configuration)
Format      : 16-bit PCM WAV, 22050 Hz, mono
Naming      : real/ LJxxx-xxxx.wav
              fake/ LJxxx-xxxx_gen.wav  (_gen suffix = generated)
